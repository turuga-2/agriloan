About Agriloan System

We offer loans to farmers in form of goods as opposed to other loaning systems which loan out cash. Our Goal is to help the farmers both large and small scale farmers to achieve their dreams of good yeilds without worrying about the payment upfront. The system is to serve the farmers in UasinGishu and TransNzoia counties. We have the best packaged planting seeds, fertilisers and agrochemicals that is herbbicides and pesticides.

Our loans are considerate to farmers and we offer a long repayment period of 6 months which is the same as the whole season until harvesting. This will give allow our farmers enough time to sell their produce and get enough money to repay the loans. In the mean time the farmers are required to pay back the interest in the 5 months. Failure to meet the repayment deadline, a penalty of 50ksh is added to the farmers' loan total. The farmer also gets a bad credit score.
